Module Nine: Identifying Your Need
Creating a civil working environment begins with knowing what
exactly are the needs of the organization and its employees. While
general principles about civility can always be created, different
companies have different needs. In this module, you will be
presented with tools you can use to identify the need for civility in
your organization. These tools are: observation, focus groups, and
anonymous surveys.
